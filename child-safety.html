Child Safety Standards

Our dating application is strictly intended for adults aged 18 years and above. We are committed to protecting children and preventing any form of child sexual abuse or exploitation (CSAE).

Age Restriction

This app is not designed for children.

Users must be at least 18 years old to register and use the app.

We use age-gating measures to restrict access by minors.

Reporting & Enforcement

Users can report profiles suspected to involve minors by contacting us directly.

Users can report messages and in-app content using the reporting feature available within the app.

Any account found to be associated with underage users or content involving minors will be immediately removed and permanently banned.

Content related to child sexual abuse or exploitation is reported to relevant authorities in accordance with applicable laws.
